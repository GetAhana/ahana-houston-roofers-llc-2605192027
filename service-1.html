Roof replacement in Houston

A roof replacement is one of the biggest home investments you'll make. Our crews handle full tear-offs and complete re-roofs with manufacturer-backed shingles, metal, or low-slope systems suited to Houston's weather.

Professional replacement specs and materials

  • Full tear-off and haul-away of old roofing materials
  • Ice-and-water shield in vulnerable valleys and eaves
  • Attic ventilation assessment and upgrade options
  • Manufacturer-backed shingles, metal, or low-slope systems with product registration

Houston Roofers has completed over 50 roof replacements across Houston, Pearland, Sugar Land, and surrounding neighborhoods since 2008.

How we handle Roof replacement

Houston Roofers roof replacement follows a clear three-step process: honest estimate, professional installation, and full documentation.

  • Free in-person estimate and material options — We arrive on schedule, inspect your entire roof and attic, and walk you through shingle grades, colors, and warranty options. You receive a written quote with timeline and labor scope before we commit to anything.
  • Scheduled tearoff, installation, and cleanup — On the scheduled date, our crew removes old materials safely, installs new underlayment and flashing, lays your new roof, and performs a final magnet sweep. We protect landscaping and leave your property cleaner than we found it.
  • Final walkthrough, documentation, and warranty registration — Before final payment, you inspect the completed roof with our crew. We provide photos, invoice, and manufacturer product registration information so your warranty is active from day one.
When to call us

A roof replacement becomes necessary when repairs no longer make sense. Look for these warning signs that it's time for a new roof.

  • Multiple leaks or water stains in different rooms—a sign of widespread underlayment failure.
  • Granule loss (dark spots or bald patches) visible on shingles or in gutters after storms.
  • Sagging ridge line or missing shingles across large sections of the roof.
  • Roof age over 20 years (most asphalt shingles last 15–20 in Houston's heat and humidity).
  • Storm damage (hail or wind) affecting more than 25% of the roof surface.

FAQ — Roof replacement

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential tear-offs and re-roofs take one to three business days depending on roof size, complexity, and weather. We'll provide a realistic timeline in your written estimate.

What warranty comes with a new roof?

Houston Roofers backs all labor with a two-year workmanship warranty. Manufacturer material warranties (typically 10–30 years) apply when you register products per the label. We handle all registration paperwork.

Can you work around my schedule?

Yes. Once you approve the estimate, we schedule the replacement at a time that works for you. We'll coordinate access, parking, and cleanup to minimize disruption to your home and neighbors.
