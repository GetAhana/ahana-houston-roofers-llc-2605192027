Services

Services for Houston Homeowners

Houston Roofers handles residential and commercial roofing across Houston and the surrounding areas. Whether you need an emergency leak repair, storm damage assessment, a full roof replacement, or routine inspection and maintenance, we deliver honest assessments, clear pricing, and photo-documented work. Every job is backed by our two-year workmanship warranty and the expertise of a crew with 18+ years in the trade.

Our flagship services

What we do best

These three services drive the most calls in Houston. Each one includes a free estimate, detailed inspection, and photo documentation. Click any card below to learn more about the process, timelines, and guarantees.

Roof replacement

Full tear-offs and re-roofs with manufacturer-backed systems across Houston. We handle attic ventilation upgrades, complete underlayment, and a final magnet sweep before leaving your property. Every replacement includes a written estimate, timeline, and our two-year workmanship warranty.

Learn more

Roof repair

Leak repairs, flashing fixes, and shingle replacement across Houston and nearby areas. We trace leaks to their source, repair or replace damaged sections, and provide emergency tarping for active storms. Every repair includes photo documentation and a warranty on our workmanship.

Learn more

Storm damage

Hail and wind inspections with full photo documentation for your insurance adjuster. We provide honest repair-versus-replace guidance and file insurance paperwork support to get your claim approved. No pressure—just facts and expertise.

Learn more

Commercial roofing

Low-slope repairs, coating maintenance, and multi-property management plans for retail and light industrial properties. We offer quick turnarounds, minimal disruption, and documented service that protects your bottom line.

Learn more

Gutters

Seamless aluminum gutter systems, downspout routing, and leaf guards engineered to handle Texas downpours. Proper drainage protects your foundation, landscaping, and roof system from water damage.

Learn more

Roof inspections

Pre-listing home inspections, post-storm assessments, and annual roof tune-ups with written photo reports. Know the condition of your roof before buying, selling, or filing an insurance claim.
