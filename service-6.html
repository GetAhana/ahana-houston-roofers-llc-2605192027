Roof inspections

Professional roof inspection in Houston

A professional roof inspection reveals hidden damage, documents condition before major decisions, and helps you plan maintenance or repairs. Houston Roofers provides detailed written reports with photos for home sales, insurance claims, and routine maintenance planning.

Comprehensive inspection scope and reporting

  • Full exterior roof inspection including shingles, flashing, gutters, vents, and chimney
  • Attic inspection for moisture, mold, ventilation, and structural condition
  • Photo documentation of condition, damage, and maintenance needs
  • Written report with findings, repair recommendations, and estimated cost ranges

Houston Roofers has completed hundreds of detailed roof inspections for homebuyers, sellers, and insurance claims across Houston and surrounding areas.

How we handle Roof inspections

Houston Roofers roof inspection follows a transparent three-step process: thorough assessment, detailed reporting, and supportive next steps.

  • Scheduled site visit and thorough inspection — Our inspector arrives on time, examines the roof exterior, checks the attic interior, and documents findings with photos. The visit typically takes 45 minutes to an hour.
  • Written report with photos and recommendations — You receive a detailed report showing roof age, condition, visible damage or wear, maintenance recommendations, and estimated costs for repairs. Photos are included to illustrate key findings.
  • Follow-up support and repair referrals — If the inspection finds issues, we provide honest guidance on urgency and repair options. We can schedule repairs directly or provide estimates so you're informed for your real estate or insurance decisions.
When to call us

An inspection is recommended before selling, after storms, or every 2–3 years as preventive maintenance. These scenarios make professional assessment especially valuable.

  • Selling your home—an inspection report shows buyers the roof's true condition and your transparency.
  • Buying a home—an inspection protects you from inheriting major roof damage or surprise repairs.
  • After a major storm—documentation helps insurance claims and identifies damage before it gets worse.
  • Roof is over 15 years old—annual inspections catch wear early and guide maintenance decisions.
  • Receiving insurance or warranty information—baseline inspections document condition at a specific point in time.

FAQ — Roof inspections

How much does a roof inspection cost?

Roof inspections are typically $200–$400 depending on roof size and complexity. We provide a quote upfront. Many homebuyers and sellers find the investment valuable for informed decisions.

How long does an inspection take?

Most inspections take 45 minutes to an hour. We examine the roof exterior, check the attic, and document findings with photos. You receive the written report within 1–2 business days.

Can an inspection be used for insurance or real estate purposes?

Yes. Our inspection reports are detailed and photo-documented, suitable for insurance companies, home sales transactions, and warranty claims. We include all findings and professional recommendations.
