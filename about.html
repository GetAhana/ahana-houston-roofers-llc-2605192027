Our story

Our story in
Houston

We started Houston Roofers on a family roofing legacy dating back to the 1990s. Our founder and team saw an opportunity to give homeowners and businesses one trusted crew for repairs, storm damage, and full system replacements.

From our first job to our 52nd five-star review, we've built our reputation on showing up on time, explaining options clearly, and completing work the right way. Every roof we touch gets the same attention to detail and respect for your property.

Today, we serve Houston and surrounding communities with the same values: integrity, excellence, honest pricing, and respect for your home. Whether it's a leak, a storm claim, or a new roof, you get a crew that listens and delivers.

FAQs

Common
questions

Do you charge for estimates?
We offer free, no-pressure estimates on all roofing work—repairs, replacements, inspections, and storm assessments. We arrive on time, document what we find with photos, and send you a written quote before any work begins.
How fast can you respond to an emergency?
Yes. We prioritize storm and leak calls when scheduling allows, and we aim for same-week site visits on most requests. You'll get a realistic arrival window when you call or text.
What warranty do you offer?
We stand behind our work with a two-year labor warranty on workmanship. Manufacturer material warranties apply when you register products per the label. Call us with your invoice number if you ever have a concern.
