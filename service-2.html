Roof repair in Houston

A leaky roof doesn't always need replacement—often a targeted repair saves money and extends your roof's life. Houston Roofers traces leaks, fixes flashing, replaces damaged shingles, and handles emergency tarping during storms.

Professional repair techniques and materials

  • Leak detection and source tracing using water testing and attic inspection
  • Flashing replacement around chimneys, vents, and skylights with proper sealing
  • Shingle repair or full-section replacement to match existing roof
  • Emergency tarping and temporary fixes available for active leaks during storms

Houston Roofers responds to hundreds of leak and repair calls each year across Houston, Pearland, Sugar Land, Katy, and The Woodlands with same-week availability.

How we handle Roof repair

Houston Roofers roof repair follows a three-step process: fast inspection, targeted repair, and complete documentation.

  • Same-week leak inspection and damage assessment — Call or text to schedule a site visit. Our crew inspects the roof from above and the attic from inside, traces the leak source, and takes photos to document the damage and moisture.
  • Repair work and temporary protection if needed — For active leaks, we provide emergency tarping to stop water entry immediately. Then we repair or replace the damaged area—flashing, shingles, or underlayment—and verify the fix with a water test if appropriate.
  • Photo documentation and warranty confirmation — We send you before-and-after photos, a detailed invoice, and warranty terms in writing. If the leak recurs within two years due to our workmanship, we'll return and fix it at no charge.
When to call us

Water stains, mold, or damp insulation in your attic are clear signs of a roof leak. Early detection prevents expensive structural damage.

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls, especially after heavy rain or storms.
  • Damp or soft spots in attic insulation when inspected from inside.
  • Mold growth or musty smell in the attic or top-floor closets.
  • Missing, cracked, or curled shingles near the leak location.
  • Rust stains on nails or fasteners visible inside the attic.

FAQ — Roof repair

How quickly can you fix a leak?

We aim for same-week site visits on all repair requests. For active leaks during storms, emergency tarping is available to stop water entry immediately, with permanent repair scheduled shortly after.

Will you warranty the repair if the leak comes back?

Yes. Houston Roofers backs all leak repairs with a two-year workmanship warranty. If water returns due to our repair, we'll fix it at no charge with your invoice number.

How do you find a leak source when it's not obvious?

We inspect the roof and attic carefully, trace water flow patterns, check for missing or damaged shingles and flashing, and sometimes use water testing to pinpoint the entry point. We document findings with photos before recommending a repair plan.
