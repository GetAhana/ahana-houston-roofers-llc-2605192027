Commercial roofing services in Houston

Commercial flat and low-slope roofs face unique challenges in Houston's heat, humidity, and storm season. Houston Roofers provides targeted repairs, protective coatings, and preventive maintenance plans to extend roof life and minimize downtime.

Commercial roofing systems and service scope

  • TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen membrane repair and patch work
  • Protective coatings and reflective finishes to extend roof life and reduce cooling costs
  • Preventive maintenance plans with scheduled inspections and minor repairs included
  • Emergency response for roof leaks and storm damage on multi-property accounts

Houston Roofers manages roofing maintenance for over a dozen retail and light industrial properties across Houston and Pearland.

How we handle Commercial roofing

Houston Roofers commercial roofing service follows a preventive approach: assessment, scheduled maintenance, and documented partnership.

  • Initial roof assessment and preventive plan — We inspect your commercial roof(s), document condition, and recommend a preventive maintenance schedule. For multi-property accounts, we coordinate timing to minimize operational disruption.
  • Scheduled maintenance and repair work — We perform repairs, apply protective coatings, seal seams, and clear drainage pathways according to your plan. Work is scheduled during off-hours when possible, and we provide daily status updates.
  • Documentation and ongoing partnership — Every service is documented with photos and reports. We track maintenance history and adjust the plan based on performance, helping you avoid surprise failures and budget confidently for roof upkeep.
When to call us

Commercial roofs require regular attention. Watch for these signs that repairs or maintenance may prevent costly system failure.

  • Ponding water (standing water pools) in roof valleys after rain—a sign of settling or poor drainage.
  • Blistering, cracking, or peeling membrane surface from UV exposure and weather cycles.
  • Seam separation or tape failure around flashing, skylights, or HVAC penetrations.
  • Interior water stains or wet insulation indicating active leaks in occupied spaces below.
  • Age over 15 years without coating—original materials are approaching end of serviceable life.

FAQ — Commercial roofing

Can you work on our roof without closing our business?

Yes. We schedule maintenance and repairs during off-hours, early mornings, or weekends when possible. For emergencies, we coordinate with your management to minimize customer impact.

Do you offer preventive maintenance plans for multi-property accounts?

Absolutely. We assess all your properties, create a customized maintenance schedule, and coordinate work timing. Plans include scheduled inspections, minor repairs, and emergency response as needed.

How does a protective coating extend roof life?

Coatings seal the membrane surface, protecting it from UV rays, weathering, and thermal stress. They also improve reflectivity, reducing cooling costs. Most coatings add 10+ years of life to an aging roof.
